How We Provide Support to American & Canadian Companies throughout Asia

Dezan Shira & Associates is a leading foreign investment (FDI) advisory firm in Asia. We assist US and Canadian businesses set up, grow, and operate in the region. Our multi-disciplinary professionals have provided a range of services that facilitate business success for over 30 years from our 29 offices and 13 Asian Alliance partner offices in APAC.

Clients can obtain comprehensive, integrated business solutions from our combined capabilities as a CPA, law firm, IT service provider, and business intelligence supplier. These blended and tailored services assist clients throughout the whole process of their investment, from site selection and corporate establishment to post-establishment operational and administrative support.

Over one million US and Canadian readers also gain knowledge on the business environments of Asia through our publishing subsidiary, Asia Briefing, and its briefing sites – China Briefing, Vietnam Briefing, India Briefing and ASEAN Briefing – every year.

Our Clients

Our clients consist of mid-cap companies, subsidiaries of listed corporations, smaller family firms and larger multinational enterprises. We service thousands of corporate clients, headquartered in more than 100 countries around the globe, with 40% of our clientele headquartered in North America.

While many of our clients are Fortune 500, we are as comfortable with entrepreneurs as we are with well-established and scaling businesses. Our diversified clientele allows us to strengthen the knowledge base that we continue to use in assisting our clients do business across Asia.

Our industry expertise has developed from over 30 years of supporting clients from across numerous industry sectors. Specialist and growth sectors include:

Our Offices

Our Network

We are members of numerous North American organizations that help facilitate trade and investment in Asia – whether physically located in Asia or North America – and cooperate with many more associations, economic development units, and governmental agencies.

Our Services

We are a pan-Asia, multi-disciplinary professional services firm, providing market entry, legal, accounting, tax, HR, technology, and operational advisory services tailored to help businesses and investors manage the unique hurdles of international business, across jurisdictions, and in each market.

Pre-Entry Intelligence and Advisory

Business intelligence is a critical component of the decision-making process when considering where and how an investment in Asia should be made. Our cross-regional Asia team can help your business with its strategic direction.

M&A and Due Diligence

Each M&A transaction requires a unique approach to set-up procedures, regulatory barriers, as well as the required due diligence considerations to close the deal and set the stage for future business growth with minimized risk.

Corporate Establishment and Legal Advisory

The incorporation and structure of your investment define the early stages of your Asia expansion and impact your future success. A well-planned legal structure can help to make the difference.

Tax and Accounting

Tax laws and regulations throughout Asia are continuously being updated and refined while international accounting standards vary greatly in practice. Our team of professionals is accustomed to navigating this fast-paced environment and providing local expertise.

HR, Payroll and Global Staffing / PEO

Managing personnel in Asia requires a robust approach to local regulations and customs. Our services are designed to help foreign-invested companies with compliance and best practices.

Technology and Digital Transformation

We understand that Asia’s Information Technology (IT) environment is unique and encompasses the need to understand different languages, regulations, and software. Our advisors can help you find the digital solution you need.
